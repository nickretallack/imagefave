ImageFave

ImageFave helps you collect images you like from around the web, and discover other images you might like.

To begin, drag this link into your bookmarks bar: . Then, browse the internet. When you see an image you like, click the bookmark and use the nifty UI that shows up to select the images you like.

Your user ID shall be . It is included in the bookmarklet.

I recommend using Google Browser Sync to keep this bookmark the same on different machines/browsers. Or you could store your user id in a safe place and re-insert it into a fresh bookmarklet when needed.
